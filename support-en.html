‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?

1. Enter a number in the input field
2. Select the current base (Binary, Octal, Decimal, etc.)
3. The app automatically displays results in all other bases
4. Tap any result to copy it to the clipboard

Which number systems are supported?

  • Binary (Base 2) - Uses digits 0-1
  • Octal (Base 8) - Uses digits 0-7
  • Decimal (Base 10) - Uses digits 0-9
  • Hexadecimal (Base 16) - Uses digits 0-9, A-F
  • Base32 - Uses digits 0-9, A-V
  • Base36 - Uses digits 0-9, A-Z

How do I copy results?

Tap any conversion result to automatically copy it to the clipboard. You can toggle prefix inclusion (0x, 0b) in settings.

How do I change the language?

Tap the settings icon in the top-right corner, select "Language", and choose your preferred language. The app supports English, Chinese, Japanese, Portuguese, Spanish, and Russian.

How do I switch between dark and light mode?

Tap the settings icon in the top-right corner, select "Theme", and choose Automatic (follows system), Light, or Dark mode.

Does the app collect my data?

No. Radix Converter runs entirely on your device, doesn't collect any personal information, and requires no internet connection. See our Privacy Policy for details.
